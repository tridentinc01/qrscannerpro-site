Features

Everything you need to scan

Built with Google ML Kit and CameraX for the fastest, most reliable scanning experience.

📷

Instant Scanning

Point your camera and results appear instantly. Supports all major barcode formats including QR Code, EAN-13, UPC-A, Code 128, Data Matrix, PDF-417 and Aztec.

✨

QR Code Generator

Generate QR codes for URLs, contacts (vCard), WiFi credentials, plain text, email, phone, SMS, GPS locations and App Store links.

📋

Smart Scan History

Every scan is saved automatically with a timestamp. Search, filter, favourite and export your complete history as CSV.

🔄

Batch Scan Mode

Scan multiple codes in one session without interruption. Perfect for inventory management, event check-in and product catalogues.

🔦

Flashlight Support

One tap to toggle the flashlight for scanning in dark environments. Automatically turned off when you leave the scanner.

🔒

Privacy First

No account required. No scans are uploaded to any server. Everything stays entirely on your device at all times.

📶

WiFi Connect

Scan a WiFi QR code and get prompted to join the network instantly on Android 10 and above, no typing required.

🌙

Dark Mode

Full dark mode support that follows your system preference automatically. Easy on the eyes in any lighting condition.

📤

Share and Export

Copy, share or open any scan result with one tap. Export your full scan history as a CSV file for use in spreadsheets.
